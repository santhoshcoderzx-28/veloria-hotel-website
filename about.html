Our Story

Heritage, warmth, and a relentless pursuit of excellence

Est. 1998

Born from a Passion
for Hospitality

Veloria Grand was founded by Mr. Arvind Malhotra with a singular vision: to create a hotel that felt less like a stop and more like a destination in itself. Opening its doors in 1998 with just 40 rooms, the hotel has grown into a 147-room landmark that retains the personal touch of a boutique property while offering the facilities of a world-class resort.

Every renovation, every hire, every menu change is guided by a simple principle: would a discerning guest notice the difference? If the answer is yes, we do it. If not, we go back to the drawing board.

Today, Veloria Grand is recognised by Condé Nast Traveller, Forbes Travel Guide, and TripAdvisor as one of India's finest independent luxury hotels.

Frequently Asked Questions

Standard check-in is at 2:00 PM and check-out is at 12:00 noon. Early check-in and late check-out can be arranged based on availability, and are complimentary for suite guests.

Complimentary buffet breakfast at The Veloria Dining Room is included in all Junior Suite and Presidential Suite bookings. Breakfast can be added to Deluxe Room rates for ₹950 per person per day.

We welcome well-behaved pets in our Deluxe Rooms and selected suites. A refundable pet deposit of ₹2,000 is required. Please inform us at the time of booking.

Yes, complimentary valet parking is available for all hotel guests. Our secure basement parking can accommodate up to 90 vehicles.

Veloria Grand is approximately 28 km from Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj International Airport (roughly 45–60 minutes by road). We offer airport transfer services; please contact our concierge to arrange.
